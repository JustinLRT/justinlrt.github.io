This simple web app helps you visualize how well-travelled you are in LRT-1.

How to use

The map is divided into the 20 LRT-1 stations. All you have to do is to find the station in the left and choose the appropriate level of travel in the right.

Here's what each level represents:
  • Lived near - Station you use because you live/rent.
  • Worked/studied near - Station used in your work or school today or previously.
  • Stayed near - station you used at the place where you checked in
  • Went here - station you used because of the mall, date, shopping, etc.
  • Passed by here - station passing by your train but never getting off here.
  • Never been here - station you've never been to or even passed by your train.

Your total score will be updated accordingly. You can then save the image and share on your social media.
