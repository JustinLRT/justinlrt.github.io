After the frequent updates of LRT-1 and Pasay City West High School, we started to create own content about that. Our homes became filled with talk of train ride, trainspotting, updates, announcements, schools, and many more.

We wanted to save ourselves the trouble of insufficient followers and views for people to view about LRT-1 and PCWHS, so we came up with the idea to create a website with everything that we had looked about the LRT-1 and PCWHS. So we got the people and students involved and created justinlrt.github.io.
