PCWHS National Reading Month Celebration!


Pasay City West High School embraced the spirit of literacy by launching the National Reading Month celebration at the school facade on November 13, 2023.

Attendees included the School Principal, Mr. Agapito Teodoro N. Manaog, Assistant Principal Mrs. Ivy A. Tanglao, department heads, teachers, and Grade 10 learners.

Mr. Manaog, the school principal, delivered a powerful and inspiring message, urging all teachers and students to weave the magic of reading in the classrooms. He emphasized the pivotal role of reading comprehension in enhancing learners' understanding and acquiring essential competencies.

Opening remarks by Mrs. Betinna Amiscaray, Head of the Filipino Department, and closing remarks by Mrs. Veronica Miranda, English Department Head, added to the event's significance. Mr. Dan Sajorda, the event chairperson, outlined exciting activities, including a captivating reading by Mrs. Grace R. Legada.

Grade 9-Lavoisier, led by Mrs. Celia L. Singcol, delivered a choric speech on Shakespeare's “Seven Ages of Man.” Meanwhile, Grade 8 Mendel and Aristotle, under the guidance of Mrs. Raquel May Basa, presented their interpretation of Ecku Mcgred's “I am an African child.” Kudos to the students and their trainers for these impressive performances!

Ezekiel Pilon and Alexandra Sophia Jimenez charmed as emcees, ensuring an engaging celebration.

Good news for our Pasay Westians!


Bukas na ang canteen sa Building 3 ground level pagkatapos ng higit na 4 na buwan sa pagsara dahil sa pagpapaayos nito.

Matatandaan natin na noong June 1, 2023 ay temporayo itong isinasara upang magsasagawa ng pagpapaayos nito. Ang canteen ay temporayo muna malipat sa likurang bahagi ng gymnasium.

Sa ngayon, maayos, malinis, bagong pintura, at marami pang improvements at pagbabago sa canteen na ito.

Keep it up Pasay City West High School for maintaining our school to make students feel better.
