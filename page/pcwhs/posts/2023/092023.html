PCWHS Parent-Teacher Association Welcomes New Elected Officers!


With the unwavering support of our dedicated school principal, Mr. Agapito Teodoro N. Manaog Principal IV, we are thrilled to announce the successful oath-taking ceremony of the newly elected SPTA – BOD Officers for the School Year 2023 – 2024. This momentous event took place on September 25, 2023, right here at the Pasay City West High School conference room.

Let's give a big round of applause to our newly elected SPTA officers who will be working tirelessly to support our school community:
  • President: Mr. Fernando E. Rivera
  • Vice President: Mrs. Ana Hernandez
  • Secretary: Mrs. Herminia L. Balantakbo
  • Treasurer: Marissa M. Famorcan
  • Collection and Disbursing Officer: Mrs. Marie Fe B. Lizardo

But that's not all! We also have dedicated Grade Level Representatives and Alternates who will be the voice of their respective classes:
Grade Level Representatives and Alternates:
  • Grade 7: Jennifer P. Ofdi and Allan A. Bornales
  • Grade 8: Marife C. Soral and Maria Elsa A. Carongoy
  • Grade 9: Marissa M. Famorcan and Elnora Develos
  • Grade 10: Ana Hernandez and Corazon Baltazar
  • Grade 11: Fernando E. Rivera and Jenalyn B. Carreon
  • Grade 12: Marife B. Lizardo and Ryan C. Pontijes

We're incredibly excited to see the positive impact these dedicated individuals will have on our school community. Join us in congratulating them and wishing them a successful and productive year ahead!

Maynilad’s Wastewater Seminar at PCWHS: Promoting Environment Awareness


Maynilad Water Services, Inc. conducted an informative seminar on wastewater management at the Pasay City West High School Audio Visual Room (AVR) on September 27, 2023.

Selected participants, including students from grades 8, 10, and 12, along with members of the SSLG, actively participated in the morning session, while students from grades 7, 9, and 11, participated in the afternoon session.


Pasay City West High School
September 28, 2023 at 12:53 PM
PCWHS Administrators, Teachers, and Students Commit to Environmental Stewardship at Science Month 2023 Opening Ceremony on September 27, 2023 at PCWHS Gymnasium. The theme of the said event is Siyensa, Teknolohiya at Inobasyon: Kabalikat sa Matatag, Maginhawa at Panatag na Kinabukasan.

Administrators, teachers, and students came together to sign a manifesto, reaffirming their commitment to environmental preservation. This manifesto, titled 'PCWHS Commitment for the Environment,' outlines specific actions that both students and teachers will undertake. These actions aim to minimize the impact of their activities on future generations, promote sustainable development through environmental protection, and emphasize social responsibility.
