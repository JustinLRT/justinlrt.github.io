Light Rail Transit Line 1


The official song was released on February 21, 2024. It aims to encourage commuters to take the LRT-1, highlighting its fast transit times, cold air conditioning, and scenic outdoor views. Justin frequently rides the LRT-1 because of its excellent service, and he even offers free rides to students who show good behavior.

The line "Masaya ako pag may pasok" reflects the joy students feel during school days when they can catch up with classmates, friends, and teachers, as well as the excitement of traveling and riding the LRT-1 together.

The song achieved over 68k views on Facebook, inspiring many viewers to experience the reliable services of the LRT-1.

Kinupit Dahil Sa Korapsyon


Released on December 1, 2025, this track marks the first protest-themed song in Justin's discography. It promotes accountability and transparency while speaking out against the actions of corrupt public officials. The lyrics focus on heavy taxation, excessive fees, systemic disasters, the sacrifices made by ordinary citizens, and the collective voice protesting against dishonest leadership.

This song was also featured at Justin's high school as an instructional tool to assess and engage HUMSS students.
