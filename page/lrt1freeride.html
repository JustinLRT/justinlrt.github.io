Justin offered the free ride for students of Pasay City West High School who ride LRT-1 going to Baclaran station. The offer is implemented to help students to save their spending for LRT-1 ride to save their money to buy their goods due to fare hike implemented to all railway lines. This free ride runs from April 1 to November 28, 2023.

Students can meet with Justin to avail to enjoy free ride and not only that, students can feel the cold air conditioning, fast speeds, beautiful outside view and convenient.

How to avail free ride

  • Step 1 - you need to meet Justin at Libertad station southbound (going to Baclaran).
  • Step 2 - write your complete name, grade and section and station destination in the logbook.
  • Step 3 - wait for some time to process your ticket without paying.
  • Step 4 - get your ticket and enjoy your free ride.

There's no limit how many free rides do you want as long as you're a student of Pasay City West High School and going to Baclaran from Libertad.

Reminder

Free ride is offered by Justin. Do not tell tellers and staffs of LRT-1 if free ride is available because this offer can get from Justin.

FAQ

Question Answer
Who can avail the free ride? Free ride is only available for students of Pasay City West High School.
Where is only available? It is only available at Libertad station southbound only (going to Baclaran).
Is free ride mandatory? No, if students want free ride then they come with me and if they want to pay with own money then they pay it. This offer is not mandatory as the students want free ride or not.
I don't meet Justin at given place, what should I do? You need to wait at Libertad station southbound, or possibly can't ride LRT right now so you need to pay with your own money.
When the free ride is implemented? The free ride starts from April 1 to November 28, 2023.
Is this offer affiliated with the LRTA and LRMC? No, this offer is not affiliated with Light Rail Transit Authority and Light Rail Manila Corporation and they don't offer free rides in LRT-1 since the line is privatized.
