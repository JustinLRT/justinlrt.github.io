Recommended formats

While advertising is very important to us to promote your business, product, brand, etc. to make the viewers see your content. You may choose one per slot to advertise.

Image

  • Resolution: 720×720 px minimum, 2500×2500 px maximum
  • Aspect ratio: 1:1, 3:2, 4:3, 5:3, and 16:9
  • Format: JPEG and PNG
  • Image must be attractive to viewers

Video

  • Resolution: 360p minimum, 720p maximum
  • Aspect ratio: 1:1, 3:2, 4:3, 5:3, and 16:9
  • Format: MP4
  • Video must not exceed to 30 seconds

Text

  • Maximum of 100 characters
  • The text must be attractive
  • * The font and background color can be changed depending on your preferences.

If you choose image or video, it is recommended to upload it to Google Drive to prevent quality loss and send us a link.

Ad duration and prices

The minimum duration for advertising is 1 day. There is no maximum duration to run your ad in the site ad long as you comply with the requirements.

Monthly rental per ad slot is ₱20 per month. See the table below for complete computation.

Duration Price per ad slot

1 to 7 days ₱10 ₱5
8 to 14 days ₱10

In monthly basis, only the same day of the month when the campaign start and as the end. One month is 28/29/30/31 days (depending on the month and situation). Examples are:
  • January 1 as starting date will be end in the 1st day of any month
  • April 19 as starting date will be end in the 19th day of any month
  • December 27 as starting date will be end in the 27th day of any month
  • March 28 as starting date will be end in the 28th day of any month
  • October 29 as starting date will be end in the 29th day of any month except February which end in 28th day in common year
  • June 30 as starting date will be end in the 30th day of any month except February which end in 28/29th day.
  • May 31 as starting date will be end in the 31st day of January, March, May, July, August, October, and December; 30th day of April, June, September, and November; and 28/29th day of February
