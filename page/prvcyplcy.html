Our Commitment to You

Justin LRT is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. It is important for you to understand what Personal Information we will collect, how we will use it and who may see it.

What is Personal Information?

Personal Information is information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person. An identifiable natural person is one who can be identified, directly or indirectly, by reference to an identifier such as a name, an identification number, location data, an online identifier, or to one or more factors specific to the physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural or social identity of that natural person.

We do not consider personal information to include information that has been anonymized or aggregated so that it can no longer be used to identify a specific natural person, whether in combination with other information or otherwise.

Personal Information Collection and Use

Justin LRT requires no account creation or user registration to use our website. We do not collect any personal data from you such as your name, home address, email address or telephone number.

By accessing our website, the external IP address of your computer as well as http specific information will be stored and logged for statistical, verification and organizational purposes. Our website is designed to be used without indication of any additional personal data.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies, like most websites on the Internet. A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent from a website and stored on your computer or mobile phone by the web browser. Cookies are used to provide a reliable mechanism allowing websites to remember information. Cookies are often used for site navigation and to improve user experience. There are two types of cookies that our website uses - first-party cookies and third-party cookies.

First-Party Cookies

We use the following first-party cookies on this website.
  • Session cookies are used for site navigation to protect ourselves from web SPAM.
  • Persistent cookies are used to store any selections you make while using our website. We do not set persistent cookies without an action from you.

None of these first-party cookies contain personally identifiable information and these cookies are considered essential cookies for the website to function properly.

Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party advertising partners that use cookies to serve advertisements on our website. These cookies are used to inform our advertising partners if an advertisement has been served to the same computer before, and if so, when the advertisement was served. These cookies are used to ensure that the advertisements you see are both relevant and appropriate.

Your Privacy Choices

By using this website, you are consenting to the placement of cookies in your browser by us and our third-party advertising partners. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully for details about why we use cookies and the information they collect from and about you.

Withdraw Your Consent at Any Time

If you do not wish to accept cookies in connection with your use of this website, you will need to delete, and block or disable cookies via your browser settings; see below for more information on how to do this. Please note that disabling cookies will affect the functionality of the website, and may prevent you from being able to access certain features.

How to Delete and Block our Cookies

Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. However, if you use your browser settings to block all cookies (including essential cookies), you may not be able to access all or parts of the functionality of the website. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, our system will issue cookies as soon as you visit the website.
