LRT-1


LRT-1 is owned by Light Rail Transit Authority and the operator and maintenance by Light Rail Manila Corporation. LRT-1 started operations in December 1, 1984 and make this a first LRT system in Southeast Asia. With the length of 19.65 kilometers and 20 stations, LRT-1 is a great service ever because the air conditioning is very cool, the speed is fast, and enjoyable view outside.     LRT-1 update

    Free ride for students of Pasay City West High School!

    Justin offered the free ride for students of Pasay City West High School who ride LRT-1 going to Baclaran station and they are Grade 8, 10, and 12. The offer is implemented to help students to save their spending for LRT-1 ride to save their money to buy their goods. This free ride runs from August 28, 2023 until June 21, 2024.

    More information about LRT-1 free ride

    Roosevelt station is now Fernando Poe Jr.


    By virtue of Republic Act No. 11608 and the Light Rail Transit Authority (LRTA) Board of Directors Resolution No. 002-2023, Light Rail Manila Corporation (LRMC) has officially renamed LRT-1 Roosevelt to Fernando Poe Jr Station in August 20, in time for the 84th birth anniversary of the "King of Philippine Movies" and National Artist for Film.
