LRT-1

Main article: LRT Line 1

  • LRT-1 Navigation Page - a direct shortcut for tracking rolling stock generations, station data, and system operators.

The LRT-1 system is officially owned by the Light Rail Transit Authority (LRTA), while its day-to-day operations and network maintenance are managed by the Light Rail Manila Corporation (LRMC). Service starts on December 1, 1984, the LRT-1 stands as the historic first light rail transit system in Southeast Asia. Spanning a 26-kilometer track network across 25 active stations, it continues to provide rapid transit options featuring crisp air conditioning, fast commute speeds, and beautiful elevated city views.

Pasay City West High School

Main article: Pasay City West High School

Pasay City West High School is a premier, large-scale public secondary educational institution in Pasay City, Philippines, established in 1949. Located along Pasadeña Street, FB Harrison, the campus accommodates a vibrant student body of over 7,500 learners under the current leadership of principal Mr. Agapito Teodoro N. Manaog.

I curated this to highlight the vibrant community, rich history, and ongoing campus milestones that make the largest public high school in Pasay City so exceptional.
